Full Infrastructure Cost Breakdown

A detailed mathematical breakdown of exactly why the system costs what it does, based on an assumed workload statistics of 5 pre/post release tasks per day with 50 pages average per project.

📊 The Workload Foundation

Every calculation below is based on this foundational workload matrix.

Metric Calculation Result

Total Monthly Data Generated 22,500 x ~3MB per video ~67.5 GB / month
Why are there two main costs?
1. GCP Compute: Charged ~$2.01/mo strictly for the CPU power to encode 450 consolidated videos.
2. Core Worker: Charged $0 to ~$25/mo to host the separate Node.js background process that listens to the queue, runs AI checks, and triggers those GCP video jobs.
